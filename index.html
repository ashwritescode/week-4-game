Crystal Collector

So how does this work?!

Check out your random number! There are 4 (count 'em FOUR) crystals below. Everytime you click on one, your points go up. Each click adds to your total score. Want to win? Make your score match that of the random number. If you go about the number, you LOSE! You won't see the value of any crystal until you click on it (and even then you'll have to do *MATH* to figure it out). Everytime the game starts, there will be a new number to meet, and your crystals will have new values. *GOOD LUCK*
